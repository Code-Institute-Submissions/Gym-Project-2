Rep Ranges

Rep ranges aren't as important as some people may make out. The general rule is to move a weight that presents a challenge to you. You will then want to perform enough reps to bring you relavitley close to failure.
I generally like to stick above 6 reps and below 15 reps as I find this to be efficient for time and provide enough stimulus for growth.

Below is a table that will give general ideas of what you can expect to lift for each rep range.
The best way to use this table is to select a weight that you are comfortable with and do as many reps as possible. Note down this weight and how many repitions you were able to perform.
